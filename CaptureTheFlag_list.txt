Capture the Flag (CTF) is a special kind of information security competitions. These serve as a great tool to learn cybersecurity skills as well as sharpen them.

Introductory:
    OverTheWire: Wargames (http://overthewire.org/wargames/)
    PicoCTF (https://picoctf.com/)
    
    
Advanced:
   Holiday Hack (https://www.holidayhackchallenge.com)
      Each year SANS Institute holds a Holiday Hack Challenge.
      The URL above will default to the most recent challenge, however you can access previous challenges by specifying the year in the URL
          Example-   https://www.holidayhackchallenge.com/2016/
